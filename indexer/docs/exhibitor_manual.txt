Exhibitor Manual

Build-up:
Exhibitors may access the venue from 08:00 on Monday.
All shell scheme stands must be complete by 18:00.

Breakdown:
Breakdown begins at 17:30 on Wednesday.
No dismantling is permitted before this time.

Deliveries:
All deliveries must be booked via the loading bay system.
Large items must arrive between 08:00 and 11:00.

Power:
Standard shell scheme includes 1 x 500W socket.
Additional power can be ordered via the exhibitor portal.
